Underfitted Social Club

A community of people passionate about machine learning. Our mission is to decentralize the field enabling individuals to make an impact and directly benefit from their contributions.

Join our Discord

Achieving our mission

We are tackling 3 fundamental challenges:

  1. Machine learning education should be accessible to everybody.
  2. A decentralized community should be a platform capable of empowering and amplifying individual voices.
  3. Individuals should be financially rewarded based on their contributions and independently of their credentials and background.

The central piece of our plan is a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO), enabling our community to self-govern with unparalleled levels of autonomy. Our community is fully owned and governed by its members.

Everybody's free to join! We believe our strength is our diversity and we embrace all levels of experience and knowledge, race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, and socio-economic background.

We are currently working on creating a system to financially reward members proportionally to their contributions to the community. Our plan is to use a crypto token as the central piece of this system. To increase the value of this token over time, we are planning to offer to the public different services built by the community.

Frequently asked questions

What are you currently working on?

We are currently working on the following:

  • Enable ownership and governance of the community using a crypto token.
  • Improve the tools to manage the community.
  • Plan the implementation and release of our first project.
I don't know much about machine learning, can I still join?

Absolutely!

Not only is this community the perfect place to get started with machine learning, but we are also looking for passionate people from different backgrounds and experiences willing to help wherever there’s a chance.

How can I join?

We are still accepting founder members, and we are planning to recognize them as soon as we finish working on our ownership and governance token.
