What's going on?

Building + Sharing

Our process is simple: we turn ideas into projects and reward members proportionally to their contributions.

We are focused on pushing the boundaries of what motivated individuals can accomplish. Every application we build is fully governed and owned by the community.

Memberships

Becoming a member

Members can vote, participate in projects and receive compensation. Plus you get a special, collectible NFT badge!

A membership takes your experience to a whole new level, but you don't need one to participate: join our Discord and start contributing!

Frequently Asked Questions

We don't have all the answers, but we do have some.

Why is this a DAO?

Because we want the community to be owned and governed by its members.

We want everyone to participate, regardless of who you are, where you live, and how much time you want to contribute.

Do I need machine learning experience?

No, you don't.

But that won't last long. You'll have a chance to work and learn from other people. Everyone can contribute and every contribution matters.

Is this a web3 thing?

Yes, it is.

But don't worry if you aren't familiar with it yet. We'll take care of that.

Is this free?

You can join the community for free.

There are member-only areas for which you need a membership access, but everything else is free.

Our community is fully owned and governed by its members. We believe our strength is our diversity, and we embrace all levels of experience and knowledge, race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, and socio-economic background.
