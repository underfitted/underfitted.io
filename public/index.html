Frequently Asked Questions

We don't have all the answers, but we do have some.

Why is this a DAO?

Because we want the community to be owned and governed by its members.

Do I need any experience?

In short: no, you don't.

One of our goals is to help you learn and build a name for yourself.

Is this a web3 thing?

Yes, it is.

But don't worry if you aren't familiar with it yet. We'll take care of that.

Is this free?

You can join the community for free.

There are member-only areas for which you need a membership access, but everything else is free.

Our community is fully owned and governed by its members. We believe our strength is our diversity, and we embrace all levels of experience and knowledge, race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, and socio-economic background.
